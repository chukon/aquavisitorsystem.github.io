Aqua Visitor System

The Aqua Visitor System is comprised of a web-based scheduling app, iPad app, and name badge printer that allows Aqua employees to schedule and visitors to check-in and check-out with a touchless experience.

Schedule a Visitor

1.       Go to aquavisitorsystem.github.io

2.       Enter in required fields (see below image #1-7). Click Schedule Visitor (see below image #8)

3.       Create email to visitor

a.       Click link: Click here to create email to guest (see below image #1)

                                                               i.      This will create a new email to guest along with scheduling information & QR Code.

                                                             ii.      The QR code will be used by guest to utilize touchless check-in by using iPad app located in the foyer area.

b.       Edit & send reminder email to guest

View/Edit Visitor Schedule

1.       Go to aquavisitorsystem.github.io & click on View Scheduled Visits (see below image #1)

2.       Enter in your Aqua Network Login ID (example: ckonkol) (see below image #1)

3.       Click blue button (see below image #2)

4.       Select schedule to edit by clicking the Click here link. (See image below #1)

5.       Edit fields.

a.       All fields are editable except for Aqua Employee Network ID (See image below #1)

b.       Click Update Visit to update schedule (See image below #2)

c.       Emal updated schedule to visitor (See image below #3)

Update Visitor Schedule Active/In-Active

By default, all schedules are active. However, there may be a schedule that is on hold or cancelled all together. In those cases, you need to make schedule In-Active.

1.       Go to aquavisitorsystem.github.io & click on View Scheduled Visits (See image below)

2.       (1) Enter in your Aqua Network Login ID (example: ckonkol) & (2) Click blue button (See image below)

3.       (1) Select schedule to make in-active/active by clicking the Click here link. (See image below)

4.       Click the link below Status to change status to active or in-active

Reports

1)      Todays visitor schedule: aquavisitorsystem.github.io/?today=now

2)      Schedules

a.       Active

                                                               i.      All Active schedules: aquavisitorsystem.github.io/?all=yes

                                                             ii.      User Active schedules: aquavisitorsystem.github.io/?Active=Yes&userid=[Login ID]

Example: aquavisitorsystem.github.io/?Active=Yes&userid=ckonkol

b.       In-Active

                                                               i.      All In-Active schedules: aquavisitorsystem.github.io/?all=no

                                                             ii.      User In-Active schedules: aquavisitorsystem.github.io/?Active=No&userid=[Login ID]

Example: aquavisitorsystem.github.io/?Active=No&userid=ckonkol

Check-In/Check-Out Using iPad App

Use the below steps to check-in or check-out!

 

1.       Tap or Scan (See image below)

a.       Have a QR Code? Great! Scan QR Code by showing to the iPad camera!

b.       Dont have a QR Code? Great! Tap the green button!

                                                              i.      Select your schedule by tapping Select link (See image below)

                                                             ii.      To Check-in or Check-out se Tap Here to Check-in/Out (See image below)

2.       Upon successful check-in get name badge from printer! (See image below)

3.       Place name badge on shirt or jacket
